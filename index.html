THE SETTING

Peppercombe Valley & the North Devon coast

Nestled in a sheltered valley within land cared for by the National Trust, our four cottages sit just steps from the pebble beach at Peppercombe and the South West Coast Path.

This is proper North Devon — dramatic cliffs, ancient woodlands, wild swimming spots and some of the best surfing beaches in the country within a short drive. The valley itself feels like a secret. You’ll hear the stream, the birds, and the sea in the distance.

OUR STORY

A place we’re honoured to share.

Hi, I’m Ash. I’ve surfed these waters my whole life and spent years working in holiday lets across the South West. Peppercombe Valley has always been special to me — it’s where I feel most at home.

After deciding to make a big life change, Sammi and I made the decision to step away from busy lives and return to North Devon. We wanted to be closer to family, to the sea, and to something that felt real. When the opportunity came to take on these four beautiful cottages under the National Trust, it felt like the right path.

We’ve poured everything into making them welcoming, comfortable and true to their heritage. We live nearby and are always happy to help with recommendations — the best places to eat, surf spots that aren’t too busy, and walks that will take your breath away.

DISCOVER NORTH DEVON

Things to do nearby

Coastal walking
The South West Coast Path starts at your doorstep. Walk to Clovelly or Hartland Point.
Surfing
Westward Ho! (15 mins), Croyde and Saunton (25–30 mins). We can advise on conditions and board hire.
Clovelly
The famous traffic-free fishing village 15 minutes away. Donkey transport and incredible harbour views.
Lundy Island
Day trips from Bideford. Seals, puffins and total wilderness. One of the highlights of any stay.
Hartland Abbey & Gardens
Stunning historic house and gardens just up the coast. Beautiful walks and a hidden cove beach.
Local food & drink
Fresh seafood, farm shops, cream teas and excellent pubs. Ask us for our current favourites.
Wildlife & rock pooling
Seals, dolphins and seabirds are regularly spotted. The beach and stream are perfect for children.
Tarka Trail & cycling
Flat, traffic-free cycling along the old railway line from Bideford. Bikes can be hired locally.
